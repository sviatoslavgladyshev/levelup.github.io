    Features of LevelUp

    • For Employers
    • For Candidates

    Finding Fit Has Never Been This Effective

    With LevelUp, employers find their ideal candidates in a matter of minutes. Instead of searching through a pile of applicants who do not align with the company’s values, LevelUp matches candidates to an employer's desired career trajectory based on the characteristics of who they are as a person.

    Employers also have the opportunity to present a holistic view of what it means to work at their firm. This extends far beyond the current position, description, and salary, to providing candidates a vision of the workplace culture, total compensation, and what a future would look like at your company.

    TRY IT

    Finding A Career Has Never Been This Easy

    Candidates using our mobile app will receive an actionable career trajectory in just 60 seconds. Instead of being purely evaluated on their resume, candidates will play simple games and define their career interests so that LevelUp can derive their core characteristics and match them with an employer.

    Once matched, candidates are shown the current position the employer needs to fill as well as the career trajectory the employer has created alongside a description of the responsibilities, compensation, and culture for all points in the career path. Candidates then have the option to accept an interview, at which point they are put in direct contact with the employer.
